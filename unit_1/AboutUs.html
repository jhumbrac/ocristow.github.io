About Us

Birds of a feather flock together, some just have a longer migration. And so was the case in 2013, when Brendan "The Tin Man" Maxim and Oliver "Turkey Boy Jenkins" Ristow, were introduced. Of the many things they had in common—notable senses of humor chief among them—it just so happened that during each of their respective hiring interviews for a job working in a ski shop in Vail, CO, they mentioned that ultimately, they dreamed of opening and operating a brewery with their closest friends. Little did either of them know that over the next seven years, they would set out to do exactly that, together.
